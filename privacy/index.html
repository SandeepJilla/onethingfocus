Privacy Policy

App: OneThingFocus
Developer: CodeWaveNative
Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

OneThingFocus does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter in the app (tasks, streaks, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (UserDefaults and App Group shared storage for the widget). Nothing leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any user-generated content outside your device

Third-Party Services

OneThingFocus does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or data processors. There are no in-app purchases or accounts required.

Children's Privacy

OneThingFocus does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date.
